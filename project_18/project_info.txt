Project Name: Weather Information Card

Description:
  - A simple card showing weather details of a city.
  - Example: "New Delhi" with temperature, weather image, and condition.
  - Clean design with centered layout.

Implementation:
  - HTML:
      * Section with city name, weather image, temperature, and short text.
  - CSS:
      * Full page centered using Flexbox.
      * White card with rounded corners and shadow effect.
      * Weather image displayed clearly.
      * Temperature highlighted in blue color.

Features:
  - Easy to read and user-friendly.
  - Can be updated for any city and weather condition.
  - Looks modern with neat design.
