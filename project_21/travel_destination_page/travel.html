Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, India. Known for its stunning views, adventure sports, and peaceful vibe, it's a favorite spot for travelers.

Top Attractions:

  • Solang Valley — Perfect for skiing and paragliding
  • Rohtang Pass — A scenic snow-covered paradise
  • Hidimba Devi Temple — Ancient wooden temple in the forest
  • Old Manali — Chill cafes, live music, and hippie vibes

Best Time to Visit

March to June and October to February

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)
