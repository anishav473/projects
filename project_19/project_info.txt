Project Name: Countdown Launch Page

Description:
  - A simple webpage that shows a countdown to a product launch date.
  - Example: "Launch on January 1, 2026".
  - Includes days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Implementation:
  - HTML:
      * Title, message, and a styled countdown section.
      * Uses <span> elements for numbers inside the timer.
  - CSS:
      * Centered layout using Flexbox.
      * Section styled with white background, black border, and shadow.
      * Countdown numbers highlighted in green boxes.
      * Clean and bold typography for easy readability.

Features:
  - Clear display of launch date and remaining time.
  - Easy to customize for any product launch.
  - Modern and minimal design.
